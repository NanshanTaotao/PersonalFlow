# Code of Conduct

PersonalFlow contributors are expected to keep the project welcoming, practical, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about risks and tradeoffs.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and do not share secrets, credentials, private prompts, local databases, or user data.
- Help maintain a collaborative environment for people with different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Sharing credentials, private logs, or user data in public channels.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after maintainers ask for a change.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants when behavior harms the project or community.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers through a private channel.
